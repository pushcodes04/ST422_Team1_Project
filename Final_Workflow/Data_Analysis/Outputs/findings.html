Caveats

  • IBRS adoption drives most of the apparent rise in raw serious-injury counts. Use the adjusted series for trend interpretation.
  • Provisional data is partial-year and reporting practice for the latest year may not be stable. Provisional-year rows are excluded from all OLS fits.
  • Boundary reorganisation excluded 52 authorities from longitudinal analysis (no record in every year of base AND recent windows).
  • Size threshold: authorities with mean KSI below 30 or fewer than 6 years of data are excluded from OLS.
