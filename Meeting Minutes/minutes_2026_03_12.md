## 3. Key decisions made

- **Decision 1:** The team will combine the three STATS19 datasets (collisions, vehicles, and casualties) to produce a final merged analytical dataset.  

  **Rationale:** The project requires a fully joined dataset so that collision locations, casualty severity, and vehicle information can be analysed together.

  **Implication:** A cleaned combined dataset (e.g., `clean_joined.csv`) will be used as the base input for hotspot identification and factor analysis.

---

- **Decision 2:** The team will interpret KSI trends using both raw data and IBRS-adjusted severity guidance from the Department for Transport.

  **Rationale:** Changes in reporting systems may affect recorded serious injury counts, so raw KSI trends alone may not accurately reflect changes in road safety.

  **Implication:** The analysis will compare raw KSI trends with IBRS-adjusted estimates to improve the reliability of conclusions.
